I am a starving artist and (occasionally) a senior at Harvard. When I'm not diligently skipping class, I write satire and essays for Teen Vogue, Harvard Political Review, and The Harvard Advocate. My animations have been featured on Malt Adult and Hellavision Television Network. Outside of that, I write for TV and film.

I have never won any kind of literary award, but I am astonishingly good at "Where's Waldo?" puzzles (if that is the sort of thing that impresses you). Other hobbies include: hunting for big sales and eating hot dogs at Central Park, although not at the same time.
